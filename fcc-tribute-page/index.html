A Tribute Page for Nikola Tesla

Nikola Tesla

One of the world's greatest inventors

Here's a time line of Nikola Tesla's life:

  • 1859 - Born to a Serbian Family in Smiljan, Tesla was nicknamed `Child of the Storm`, by his aunt. That said, his mother replied to that comment by calling him `Child of the light`.
  • 1870 - Three years later, he went back to Smiljan and shortly after his arrival; he contracted cholera and was in hospital for close to one year.
  • 1875 - Enrolled to a Polytechnic based on a military scholarship received a letter of commendation from the dean of the Technical Department.
  • 1880 - Tesla moves to Prague with the help of his uncle's money to study philosophy
  • 1881 - Began his career as Chief Electrical Engineer
  • 1882 - Goes to work for the Continental Edison Company in the section responsible for lighting systems.
  • 1885 - Met with an attorney known as Lemuel Serrel, to help in the submission of various patents.
  • 1887 - Came up with an induction motor that was operated by alternating current, a form of power that was becoming common in Europe and the USA.
  • 1890 - Researched various approaches to transmitting power by inductive and capacitive coupling. This was through the use of AC voltages that were produced by his famous `Tesla coil.`
  • 1891 - Became a naturalized citizen of the US. In the same year, he also patented his famous Tesla Coil.
  • 1943 - Dies at the age of 86.
